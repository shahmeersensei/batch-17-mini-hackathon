How Daraz Transformed Online Shopping in Pakistan

Daraz first made waves in Pakistan’s e-commerce market after its introduction in 2012. We have since grown to become Pakistan’s largest platform for online shopping with a network spread across Asia in Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, Myanmar, and Nepal. Our vision was to provide a safe, efficient online marketplace platform for vendors and customers across the country to come together.

We started off exclusively as an online fashion retail platform and over the years expanded to become a complete one-stop solution for all your buying needs. Daraz prides itself on not being just another ecommerce venture in Asia. We work tirelessly to make sure that we provide users with the best online shopping experience and value for their purchases.

Daraz is a mall, a marketplace, and a community for its customers. We have a wide selection of leading international and local brands across diverse categories including fashion, consumer electronics, household goods, beauty, sports equipment, and groceries. Our collection includes over 20 million products from over 100 categories.

Daraz offers not just a retail solution of choice for buyers, but also for sellers. With a wide and growing community of sellers, Daraz also provides a platform for entrepreneurs and small businesses to grow their reach. Sellers can create their shops, list products and grow their customer base.

Daraz is also deeply committed to supporting the local economy and empowering communities. We work to create equal opportunities and inclusive growth for all sellers and partners.

With the Daraz app, customers can enjoy a convenient and seamless shopping experience anytime, anywhere. From exclusive app-only deals and flash sales to instant notifications, our mobile platform puts the power of online shopping right at your fingertips.

Whether you're a tech-savvy shopper or someone looking for the best deals from home, Daraz is your go-to online shopping destination in Pakistan. Shop now and experience the difference.
